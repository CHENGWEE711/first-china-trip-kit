# China First Trip Launch - 7-day content calendar

Campaign: `china_first_trip_launch`

Goal: attract 100-300 first-time China travelers and measure checklist downloads,
newsletter signups, paid guide interest, purchases, Klook clicks, and contact actions.

## Guardrails

- Use only the three approved landing pages listed below.
- Use the matching URL from `utm-links.csv`; never add UTM parameters to internal links.
- Give a complete, useful answer before adding a link.
- Do not promise visa-free eligibility, payment-app approval, card acceptance, or entry.
- Check community promotion rules before posting to Reddit, Quora, or a group.
- Do not use app logos or imply affiliation with a government, payment app, or booking provider.

## Daily operating rhythm

1. Publish one primary item between 09:00 and 12:00 in the target audience's time zone.
2. Publish the supporting item 4-8 hours later.
3. Reply to genuine questions without repeating the link in every response.
4. Record the live post URL in `utm-links.csv` immediately after publishing.
5. Record 24-hour results and Day 7 totals in `weekly-report-template.csv`.
