## Website descriptions

### Full

First China Trip Kit is an independent English-language travel website designed for
first-time international visitors to China. It provides practical guidance on mobile
payments, essential apps, internet access, eSIMs, transportation, visa-free travel,
accommodation, destination planning and everyday travel preparation.

The website helps travelers prepare before departure and solve common problems after
arriving in China. Our content is primarily intended for visitors from North America,
the United Kingdom, Europe, Australia and Southeast Asia.

### Short

prepare for China, including payments, apps, internet access, transportation,
accommodation and destination planning.

### Very short

Practical China travel guides for first-time international visitors.

## General promotion method

We will promote relevant travel services through helpful, search-focused English

Our audience includes travelers preparing for international trips who may be researching
